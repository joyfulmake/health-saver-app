Privacy Policy

Flourish — Last updated: 25 June 2026

Flourish is a personal self-tracking app for habits, time, finance, and nature. This policy explains what data the app collects, where it goes, and what stays entirely on your device.

1. Data you enter

All log entries (activities, durations, notes, finance records, nature scores) are stored locally on your device using browser storage (localStorage). They are never sent to our servers. We have no access to your entries.

2. Optional cloud sync (Pro users)

If you sign in with Google and enable sync, your entries are stored in your own Google Firebase / Firestore account under your Google user ID. This data is governed by Google's Privacy Policy. We do not read, share, or process your synced data — it flows directly between your device and your Google account.

3. Google Sign-in

Sign-in is handled entirely by Google OAuth. We receive only your Google user ID and display name to associate your sync data. We do not share this with third parties.

4. Payments

Pro access payments are processed by Razorpay. Your payment details are handled entirely by Razorpay and are never seen or stored by us. See Razorpay's Privacy Policy.

5. Microphone / voice logging

Voice logging uses your device's microphone only while you are actively recording. Audio is processed on-device by your browser's built-in Web Speech API and is not sent to our servers. We do not record or store audio.

6. Push notifications

Daily reminder notifications are scheduled entirely on your device using a browser timer and the Notification API. No push server is involved. Enabling notifications does not transmit any data.

7. Feedback form

If you submit feedback through the in-app form, your message and the email address you provide are forwarded to our inbox via Web3Forms. We use this only to reply to your feedback. We do not share it with third parties or add you to any mailing list.

8. Analytics and advertising

Flourish has no analytics, no advertising, and no third-party tracking of any kind.

9. Children

Flourish is intended for users aged 13 and over. We do not knowingly collect data from children under 13. If you believe a child has provided personal information, contact us and we will delete it promptly.

10. Data deletion

  • Local data: Clear site data for flourish.is-a.dev in your browser settings, or uninstall the app.
  • Firebase sync data (Pro): Open your Firebase Console → Firestore → delete the document at users/{your-uid}/data/entries.
  • Feedback submissions: Email sriram.c76@gmail.com to request deletion.

11. Changes to this policy

Material changes will be reflected by updating the "Last updated" date above. Continued use of the app after changes constitutes acceptance.

12. Contact
